Our strategies

Intelligence That
Operates Where Work
Actually Happens

QubiSense is built for operational environments where decisions cannot wait for centralized processing. The platform operates as a distributed network of AI-powered edge nodes that analyze data locally, close to physical assets, people, and processes.

Sensors continuously generate signals across temperature, location, vibration, humidity, motion, audio, and video. Instead of transmitting raw data upstream, QubiSense performs real-time inference at the edge, enabling immediate insight and action.

Each edge node is purpose-built to handle specific intelligence tasks. Video analysis nodes perform object recognition and tracking. Audio nodes analyze voice signals and sentiment. Language nodes conduct semantic analysis on text and spoken input.

These nodes work together as a coordinated intelligence fabric, converting raw physical signals into structured, contextual understanding in real time.

Cloud systems remain an integral part of the architecture, providing orchestration, advanced analytics, long-term modeling, and enterprise system integration. However, operational decisions do not depend on continuous connectivity.

Edge nodes continue to function independently during network disruptions, synchronizing insights when connectivity is restored. This ensures continuity, resilience, and predictable performance across distributed environments.

In practical terms, this architecture enables operations to detect anomalies early, trigger corrective actions automatically, and generate auditable records without manual intervention. By blending edge-based intelligence with cloud-level oversight, QubiSense allows enterprises to move from reactive monitoring to continuous, predictive operational control at scale

Reduce Equipment Downtime with AI-Driven
Predictive Maintenance

In manufacturing and industrial automation, unplanned downtime is one of the most expensive operational risks. Industry studies estimate that unplanned downtime costs industrial manufacturers billions annually, with large facilities losing thousands of dollars per minute during production interruptions. QubiSense applies Distributed AI to detect failure signals early and convert them into timely maintenance decisions.

How Predictive Maintenance Becomes an Operational Advantage

Machines continuously generate vibration, temperature, acoustic, and electrical signals. QubiSense captures and analyzes this data at the edge, enabling real-time predictive maintenance decisions rather than waiting for centralized processing delays.

Instead of reacting to breakdowns, manufacturers gain early visibility into:

  • Bearing wear and imbalance detected through spectral vibration analysis
  • Motor overheating and electrical anomalies identified through current monitoring
  • Alignment drift and mechanical stress flagged before failure thresholds
  • Abnormal usage patterns that accelerate component degradation

By embedding Distributed AI directly into production environments, organizations can achieve measurable improvements:

  • Up to 30 percent reduction in unplanned downtime
  • 20 to 40 percent lower maintenance costs through condition-based servicing
  • Extended asset life by reducing unnecessary part replacements
  • Automated compliance documentation and maintenance logs

Maintenance teams shift from calendar-based servicing to condition-driven action. Operations leaders gain predictable production schedules and improved throughput stability.
QubiSense enables manufacturers to move from reactive repair cycles to proactive performance management, delivering the competitive edge required in modern supply chains.
